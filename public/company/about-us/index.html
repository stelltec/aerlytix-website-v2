Aerlytix is a Dublin based, technology company dedicated to the aviation finance industry. Our core product, the Analytics Suite, is the first multi-layered decision support tool delivered through the latest technologies.

The software is now used by leading lessors, global aviation banks, and investors, handling US$ billions in aircraft transactions. The Aerlytix team is assembled from the banking, technology, and aviation finance world. Aerlytix provides innovative solutions for customers to scale their businesses, all while driving efficiency and value. We are looking to lead a new era of technology in aviation finance.

As one of the fastest growing aviation technology companies in the world, we have built a reputation as a leading high-end product developer by hiring the right talent and building long term partnerships with our clients.
